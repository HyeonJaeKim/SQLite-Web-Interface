Billboard Hot 100

Source URL

The Billboard Hot 100 is the industry standard music ranking chart in the United States. The data lists the weekly top 100 singles from 8/2/1958 and 12/28/2018. This data is important because it can be used to measure the performance and popularity of performers and to make predictions about the music industry's future. By visiting my site, users can display the data based on the year, order, sort, and number of records.
